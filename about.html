just joshin’

I’m a UX/UI designer based in Chicago. I bring logic and beauty to challenging design problems. I believe in simplicity and functionality.

My interest in the idiosyncracies of human behavior enable me to bring value to user experiences in creative and meaningful ways. I dabble in code so that I can offer moments of delight to users through subtle interactions.

My previous lives as a teacher and nonprofit manager prepared me well to use empathy to define user needs, and my time at DESIGNATION further honed these skills and taught me the satisfaction that comes from bringing great ideas to life.

In my spare time, I’m a fan of all things Cleveland, backpacking, crossword puzzles, and Larry David.
