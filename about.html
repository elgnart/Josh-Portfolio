just joshin’


I’m a UX/UI designer based in Chicago. I bring logic and beauty to challenging design problems, with simplicity and functionality in mind.

My curiosity about human behavior enables me to add value to user experiences in creative and meaningful ways. I dabble in code so that I can offer moments of delight to users through subtle interactions.

My previous lives as a teacher and nonprofit manager prepared me well to use empathy to define user needs, and my time at DESIGNATION further honed these skills and taught me the satisfaction that comes from bringing great ideas to life.

In my spare time, I’m a fan of all things Cleveland, backpacking, crosswords, and Larry David.
